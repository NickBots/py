Forma Mentis network

Forma Mentis is a framework of cognitive network science that helps in interpreting language processing and the structure of knowledge embedded in textual data. By using Forma Mentis we are able to reconstruct and analyze the semantic context around the key concepts of interest and the relationships among them.

Source:

Stella, Massimo, et al. "Forma mentis networks quantify crucial differences in STEM perception between students and experts." PloS one 14.10 (2019): e0222870

NRC Lexicon

The NRC Emotion Lexicon is a multilingual list of words and their associations with eight basic emotions (anger, fear, anticipation, trust, surprise, sadness, joy, and disgust) and two sentiments (negative and positive). The annotations were manually done by crowdsourcing.

Source:

Crowdsourcing a Word-Emotion Association Lexicon, Saif Mohammad and Peter Turney, Computational Intelligence, 29 (3), 436-465, 2013
